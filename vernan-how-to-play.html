Vernan — browser client

How to play

Controls and tips for the browser version of Vernan (single-player platformer dungeon crawl). Link to this page from anywhere on the site — e.g. vernan-how-to-play.html.

Play now on Page D Play with fixed seed

1. Controls

  • Move: Arrow keys or WASD. Hold Down / S to crouch on the ground.
  • Jump: Z or Space. Jump has a short wind-up squat on the ground.
  • Attack: X — sword swing with wind-up, active, and recovery frames.
  • Subweapon: C when you have a subweapon equipped (desktop app has more subweapons).
  • Climb: Stand on a ladder tile and press Up/Down or W/S.
  • Debug: F3 toggles the collision grid overlay.
  • On touch devices, on-screen buttons appear below the game canvas.

2. The dungeon

  1. Each run is a procedurally generated graph of rooms connected by doors (left/right) and ladders (up/down).
  2. Stand at a left/right door and press Up or W to enter the neighboring room (brief fade).
  3. Room types include start, normal combat, item, shop, boss, and secret rooms.
  4. Add ?seed=NUMBER to the URL for a reproducible dungeon layout (e.g. page-d.html?seed=12345).

3. Combat

  • Crawlers hop toward you and deal contact damage. Slash with X during the active attack frame.
  • You have red heart containers shown in the bottom HUD. Invulnerability frames apply after taking damage.
  • Pick up keys and coins as you explore (full item system in the desktop app).

4. HUD

  • The bottom bar shows hearts (HP), keys, money, current room type, and run seed.
  • FPS/UPS counters appear under the canvas during play.

5. Tips & troubleshooting

  • Click the game canvas once if keyboard input does not respond (focus).
  • Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) after updates.
  • If sprites fail to load, ensure assets/vernan/ was synced from new-vernan-2/ (see repo scripts/sync-vernan-assets.sh).
  • The desktop Java game has costumes, layered Vernan body art, full boss AI, and 40+ items — the browser client is being expanded toward full parity.
