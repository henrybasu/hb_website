How to play (two players)
  1. Two players, two browsers. Each player opens this page on their own computer or phone (or use two different browsers). You cannot play both sides in one tab.
  2. Same relay URL. Both players must enter your Render relay (e.g. wss://your-relay.onrender.com/ws). The GitHub Pages site does not host a relay — leave the field empty only if you use a ?relay=… link.
  3. Host creates the room. Player 1 chooses a map and name, then clicks Host game. Copy the room code that appears and send it to Player 2 (text, chat, etc.).
  4. Guest joins. Player 2 enters that room code, the same relay URL, and their name, then clicks Join game. The match starts when both are connected.

In the match

  • Blue (P1) recruits on the left column; red (P2) on the right.
  • On your turn: pick Soldier or Gollem, then click a green square in your home column (left for Blue, right for Red). You can click Recruit first or click the green square directly.
  • Pass skips the rest of a selected piece’s actions. End turn when you are done (including skipping recruit if you do not want another unit).
  • Win by reducing the opponent to 0 gold and 0 HP on the board.
