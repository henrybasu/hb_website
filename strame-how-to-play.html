Strame Standard Online

How to play

Rules and setup for the browser version (two players, Soldier & Gollem, preset maps). Link to this page from anywhere on the site — e.g. strame-how-to-play.html.

Play now on Page C Play with relay URL in link

1. Getting connected

  1. Two players, two browsers. Each player opens the game on their own device (or use two different browsers). One tab cannot control both sides.
  2. Same relay URL. Both players must use the exact same WebSocket relay (e.g. a Render service at wss://your-relay.onrender.com/ws). The GitHub Pages site does not host a relay unless you pass one in the URL: page-c.html?relay=wss://…
  3. Host creates the room. Player 1 (Blue) picks a map, optional name, then clicks Host game. Copy the room code and send it to Player 2.
  4. Guest joins. Player 2 (Red) enters the room code, the same relay URL, optional name, then clicks Join game. The match starts when both are connected.

2. The board

  • Blue (Player 1 / Host) recruits on the left column.
  • Red (Player 2 / Guest) recruits on the right column.
  • River maps have water tiles in the middle — you cannot recruit or move onto water.
  • Each player starts with 5 gold. Recruiting costs 1 gold per unit.

3. Units (browser version)

Soldier

3 HP · 1 attack · moves 1 square (orthogonal)

Can attack orthogonally and diagonally adjacent enemies.

Gollem

5 HP · 2 attack · moves 1 square (orthogonal)

Attacks only orthogonally adjacent enemies (not diagonals).

4. Your turn

  1. Wait for the green Your turn pill. If it says the opponent’s turn, you cannot act yet.
  2. Recruit (optional, once per turn): Choose Soldier or Gollem, then click a green square in your home column. You can click Recruit first or click the green square directly.
  3. Use your pieces: Click one of your units, then Move or Attack and click a highlighted square. Each piece can move once and attack once per turn (unless it just recruited — new units skip acting that turn).
  4. Pass skips further actions for the selected piece.
  5. End turn when you are finished. You must recruit at least one unit before ending your first turn (you cannot end with zero pieces on the board).

5. Combat & gold

  • Attacking reduces enemy HP. When a unit reaches 0 HP, it is removed.
  • Killing an enemy unit grants 1 gold to the attacker’s player.
  • HP is shown on each piece on the board.

6. Winning

You win when the opponent has 0 gold and 0 HP on the board (no living units and no money left to recruit).

7. Tips & troubleshooting

  • Hard refresh the game page (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) if buttons or sprites look wrong after an update.
  • Check the note under Strame Online for the client version (e.g. v20260702-recruit2).
  • If you disconnect, use Host game or Join game again to start a new room.
  • The full desktop Strame app has many more units, maps, and modes — this page covers the thin browser client only.
